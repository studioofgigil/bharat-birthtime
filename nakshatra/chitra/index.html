Chitra Nakshatra

The brilliant — beauty, design and radiant craftsmanship.

At a glance

Ruling planet (dasha lord)Mars
Presiding deityTvashtar / Vishvakarma (the divine architect)
SymbolBright jewel or pearl
Gana (temperament)Rakshasa
Naming syllables (pada 1–4)Pe, Po, Ra, Ri
Position in zodiacNakshatra 14 of 27

Meaning & mythology

Chitra is ruled by Vishvakarma, the celestial architect who fashions the forms of the cosmos. Its symbol is a shining jewel. This is the nakshatra of design, artistry and dazzling appearance. Tradition associates Chitra with creativity, an eye for beauty and structure, charisma, a flair for the dramatic and the power to craft something brilliant and admired.

Naming syllables (Namakaran)

If a child is born under Chitra nakshatra, tradition suggests a name beginning with one of these syllables, chosen by the Moon's pada (quarter) at birth: Pe, Po, Ra, Ri — one syllable for each of the four padas.

These are the classical syllables; regional traditions vary slightly. For ritual decisions, confirm with your family panchanga or purohit.
