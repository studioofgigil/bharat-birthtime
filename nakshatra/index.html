The 27 Nakshatras

The Moon travels through 27 nakshatras — lunar mansions, or birth stars — each spanning 13°20′ of the sidereal zodiac. Your janma nakshatra is the one the Moon occupied at your birth. Explore each star's deity, ruling planet, symbol and traditional naming syllables below.
